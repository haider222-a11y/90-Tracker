PHASE 1
Build the Foundation
Days 1–21 · Apr 20–May 10 · Focus: Body + Routine + Energy
✦ Walk 10–30 min daily — barefoot on grass if possible
✦ Morning sunlight 10–15 min around sunrise
✦ Hydration: 8–10 glasses water
✦ Diet: almonds, walnuts, pistachios, fruits, fish/protein — avoid sugar, junk, cold drinks
✦ Sleep 7–8 hours, no phone 1 hour before bed
✦ Breathing Practice 5–10 min: Inhale deeply → Hold → Exhale slowly × 10–12 times
🎯 Goal: Improve energy, focus, sleep, mental clarity — physical foundation for all deeper work
✦ ✦ ✦
PHASE 2
Focus & Mind Training
Days 22–60 · May 11–Jun 18 · Focus: Concentration + Awareness
✦ Sit quietly · Eyes closed · Feet flat on ground
✦ Heartbeat awareness — feel it, stay calm, don't control it
✦ Focus training — ONE object: a dot (•), light, the moon, or the word "Allah"
✦ Visualization 5 min — light spreading from heart → whole body
✦ Mental messaging (optional) — think of someone, mentally send a thought
🎯 Goal: Improve focus, imagination, emotional awareness, ability to send/receive mental signals
✦ ✦ ✦
PHASE 3
Deep Practice & Reflection
Days 61–90 · Jun 19–Jul 18 · Focus: Silence + Awareness + Consistency
✦ Full sequence: Breathing → Heartbeat → Visualization (in order)
✦ Complete silence sitting 10–20 min — observe thoughts, feelings, inner calm
✦ Daily Telepathy journal: mood, thoughts, changes in perception, experiences
🎯 Goal: Self-awareness, emotional control, inner calm — build real, consistent telepathic sensitivity
